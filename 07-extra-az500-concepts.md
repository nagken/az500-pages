# Extra AZ-500 Concepts

> Concepts that span multiple domains or appear inconsistently in the exam outline but show up on the test.

## Defender CSPM extras

Agentless VM scan, sensitive data discovery (cloud), attack paths, governance rules - paid tier.

## Just Enough Administration

Azure RBAC custom roles + Conditional Access for admins to enforce least privilege + JIT (PIM).

## Confidential VMs / Confidential containers

AMD SEV-SNP / Intel TDX hardware-isolated trust boundary; new pattern for sensitive workloads.

## Resource locks vs RBAC

Locks (CanNotDelete/ReadOnly) prevent destructive ops even for owners; orthogonal to RBAC.
